The Four-Level System

From concrete to abstract — the science of fading

SaySee™'s four-level visual progression mirrors the stimulus fading procedures used in Applied Behavior Analysis — systematically transferring stimulus control from a real photograph to text-only as students develop symbolic understanding.

Level 1
📷
Real Photo
A teacher-captured photograph from the student's actual environment. Their real chair. Their actual cafeteria. Maximum concrete relevance.
ABA: Most-to-least prompting · Concrete stimulus
Level 2
🎨
Color Art
A colorful symbolic illustration. Introduces abstraction while maintaining strong visual clarity and color-based discrimination.
ABA: Stimulus fading · Symbolic representation
Level 3
✏️
B&W Sketch
Black and white line art removes color as a prompt, building tolerance for reduced visual information toward text.
ABA: Prompt fading · Reduced visual detail
Level 4
🔤
Text Only
The written word alone — representing full symbolic independence and emergent literacy. The ultimate goal.
ABA: Independent stimulus control · Literacy readiness

Privacy & Compliance

📄 Data Processing Agreement

SaySee LLC — Effective 2026 · For districts and educational institutions

1. Parties

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") is entered into between SaySee LLC ("Processor"), a California limited liability company located at 28 Geary St Suite 650 PMB 70334, San Francisco, CA 94108, and the subscribing educational institution ("Controller").

2. Purpose

This DPA governs the processing of student education records and personal data by SaySee LLC on behalf of the Controller in connection with the SaySee™ AAC Visual Learning Platform.

3. FERPA Compliance

SaySee LLC agrees to:

  • Use student education records only for the purpose of providing the SaySee™ service
  • Not disclose student education records to third parties without authorization
  • Maintain the confidentiality of all student education records
  • Allow the Controller to review, correct, or delete student records upon request
  • Comply with all applicable provisions of FERPA (20 U.S.C. § 1232g)

4. Data Security

SaySee LLC implements the following security measures:

  • All data encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • All data encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Private photo storage with time-limited signed URLs (7-day expiry)
  • Row-level security on all database tables
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability monitoring

5. Prohibited Uses

SaySee LLC shall not:

  • Sell student personal information to any third party
  • Use student data for targeted advertising or behavioral profiling
  • Disclose student data for any non-educational purpose
  • Retain student data beyond the termination of the service agreement
  • Transmit student identifying information to external AI services

6. AI Processing

The SaySee™ platform uses AI for semantic word matching. Before any speech transcript is processed by external AI services, all student names and identifying information are removed and replaced with anonymous placeholders. No student data is transmitted to external AI services under any circumstance.

7. Subprocessors

SaySee LLC uses the following subprocessors:

  • Supabase Inc. — Database and file storage (SOC 2 compliant)
  • Vercel Inc. — Application hosting and delivery
  • Anthropic PBC — AI semantic matching (receives anonymized data only)
  • Stripe Inc. — Payment processing (no student data shared)

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Student data is retained only while the teacher account is active. Upon account termination, all associated student data is deleted within 30 days. The Controller may request immediate deletion at any time by contacting hello@saysee.io.

9. Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting student education records, SaySee LLC will notify the Controller within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, consistent with applicable law.

10. California SOPIPA Compliance

SaySee LLC complies with the California Student Online Personal Information Protection Act. We do not sell student information, use student information for targeted advertising, or build profiles of students for non-educational purposes.

11. Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the laws of the State of California. Any disputes shall be resolved in San Francisco County, California.

Who We Are

SaySee LLC is an educational technology company based in San Francisco, California. We operate the SaySee™ AAC Visual Learning Platform at saysee.io. Contact us at hello@saysee.io.

What Data We Collect

  • Teacher account information: name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Student first names or nicknames (no last names, no IDs required)
  • Student learning level data and response history
  • Photos uploaded by teachers from their classroom environment
  • Custom vocabulary words added by teachers
  • Subscription and billing information (processed by Stripe — we do not store card numbers)

FERPA Compliance

SaySee™ is designed for FERPA compliance. We operate as a "school official" under the FERPA school official exception when used by educational institutions. Specifically:

  • Student education records are accessible only to the authorized teacher account
  • No student data is sold to any third party — ever
  • No student data is used for advertising or non-educational purposes
  • Data Processing Agreements are available for districts upon request

AI and Voice Processing

SaySee™ uses AI for semantic word matching. Before any speech is sent to AI services, all student names are removed and replaced with anonymous placeholders. No student identifying information is ever transmitted to AI services. Voice audio is never recorded or stored.

Photo Storage

Photos uploaded by teachers are stored in private, encrypted cloud storage. Photos are accessible only to the teacher who uploaded them via time-limited signed URLs. Photos are never publicly accessible.

COPPA Compliance

SaySee™ does not collect data directly from children under 13. All data is collected from and managed by teachers and school officials. We operate under the COPPA school official exception.

California SOPIPA Compliance

SaySee™ complies with California Student Online Personal Information Protection Act (SOPIPA). We do not sell student data, use student data for targeted advertising, or build profiles of students for non-educational purposes.

Data Security

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • All data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
  • Private photo storage with time-limited signed URLs
  • Row-level security on all database tables

Data Retention

Data is retained while your account is active. Upon account closure, all data is deleted within 30 days. Contact hello@saysee.io to request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time through your account settings
  • Request deletion of your data and all associated student records
  • Export your data upon request
  • Request a Data Processing Agreement for your district
