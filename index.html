The Four-Level System

From concrete to abstract — the science of fading

SaySee©'s four-level visual progression mirrors the stimulus fading procedures used in Applied Behavior Analysis — systematically transferring stimulus control from a real photograph to text-only as students develop symbolic understanding.

Level 1
📷
Real Photo
A teacher-captured photograph from the student's actual environment. Their real chair. Their actual cafeteria. Maximum concrete relevance.
ABA: Most-to-least prompting · Concrete stimulus
Level 2
🎨
Color Art
A colorful symbolic illustration. Introduces abstraction while maintaining strong visual clarity and color-based discrimination.
ABA: Stimulus fading · Symbolic representation
Level 3
✏️
B&W Sketch
Black and white line art removes color as a prompt, building tolerance for reduced visual information toward text.
ABA: Prompt fading · Reduced visual detail
Level 4
🔤
Text Only
The written word alone — representing full symbolic independence and emergent literacy. The ultimate goal.
ABA: Independent stimulus control · Literacy readiness

AI Scaffolding Engine

An autonomous co-teacher that never stops learning

SaySee©'s AI engine runs silently in the background — tracking, adjusting, and improving without any extra effort from the teacher. Student information is never accessed, stored, or transmitted by the AI. All processing uses only anonymous classroom phrases — student data never leaves the SaySee© platform.

⏱️
Response Time Measurement
A timer starts the moment an instruction is detected, measuring latency to response for each student.
🎉
Praise Detection
Detects natural praise phrases — "good job," "great work," "yes," "excellent" — and logs them as correct responses.
👤
Student Name Detection
Hears a student's name in the instruction and automatically switches to that student's profile and level.
🧠
Semantic Matching
"Take a seat" → "sit down." Understanding meaning, not just keywords — for more natural classroom use.
💡
Vocabulary Suggestions
Phrases heard frequently that don't match any word are automatically suggested for the teacher to add to the library.
🔒
FERPA-Safe AI
All student names are stripped before any AI processing. Only anonymous phrases reach external services. Student data never leaves the SaySee© platform.

Privacy & Compliance

Student data protected at every layer

SaySee© was built for schools — which means privacy isn't an afterthought, it's a design principle. Student identifying information never touches external AI services. No advertising. No data selling. Ever.

The AI does not access student information — ever. Student names, progress records, uploaded photos, and learning data are never sent to, accessed by, or processed by any AI service. The AI receives only an anonymous classroom phrase (example: "please sit down") and returns only a matching word. Nothing more.

All student profiles, progress data, and uploaded photos are stored exclusively in SaySee©'s secure, encrypted database — accessible only to the authorized teacher and district administrator. Student data never leaves the SaySee© platform.
